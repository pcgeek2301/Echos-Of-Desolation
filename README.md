🕯️ Echoes of Desolation

A psychological horror puzzle experience built in Unity, designed to immerse players in a dark, atmospheric environment filled with tension, mystery, and unsettling discovery.

🎮 Overview

Echoes of Desolation is a first-person horror puzzle game where players explore a desolate, eerie environment while solving puzzles and uncovering fragmented narrative elements. The experience focuses on atmosphere, sound design, and player-driven discovery rather than direct combat.

Players must navigate through darkness, interpret clues, and piece together the story while managing limited visibility and growing tension.

🧠 Core Features
🔦 Flashlight System
Dynamic lighting mechanic that limits visibility and enhances tension. Players must rely on it to explore and solve puzzles.
🧩 Horror Puzzle Design
Environment-driven puzzles that require observation, memory, and critical thinking.
📓 Notebook System
In-game notebook menu that tracks clues, discoveries, and narrative elements to assist player progression.
🎙️ Custom Voice Lines
Immersive audio storytelling through recorded dialogue that enhances the narrative and atmosphere.
🧭 Complete UI System
Fully implemented UI including menus, interaction prompts, and notebook interface.
🌌 Atmospheric Environment
Carefully crafted lighting, textures, and sound design to create a sense of dread and isolation.
🕹️ Gameplay
Explore a dimly lit, abandoned environment
Use your flashlight to uncover clues
Solve puzzles to progress deeper into the space
Collect and review notes in your notebook
Listen carefully—audio cues matter
🛠️ Tech Stack
Engine: Unity
Language: C#
Platform: PC (expandable to VR or mobile)
Tools:
Unity UI System
Audio system for voice lines
Custom interaction systems
🎯 Design Goals
Create a slow-burn horror experience driven by atmosphere
Encourage player curiosity and exploration
Use audio and lighting as primary storytelling tools
Deliver puzzles that feel integrated into the environment
